Overview

Rafting

Exhilaration, serenity, and a profound connection with nature blend into an extraordinary Grand Canyon white water adventure. Led by experienced guides, you'll navigate thrilling rapids, float through tranquil stretches, and learn about the rich history and geology of the Colorado River. Explore the canyon's raw beauty while you forge new friendships, embrace moments of reflection, and create lasting memories. For added convenience, many of our trips begin with a scenic flight from Las Vegas to Page, AZ Airport, offering a breathtaking aerial view of the landscape before your rafting journey begins. Depending on the itinerary, return transportation may be included, ensuring a seamless adventure from start to finish.

  • Lower Grand Canyon (4 - 6 Days)
  • Full Grand Canyon (6 - 12 Days)
